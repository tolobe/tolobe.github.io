Vote by Mail

The Chicago Board of Elections has a whole application on their site to set up your mail-in ballot. Once you get it in the mail you can fill it iout and drop it in any regualr mail box. You can also use a drop box which are found at early voting places. If you change your mind and want to vote in person that's ok! Just bring in your mail-in ballot to forfeit to the election judges at the polling place. It's all good.
